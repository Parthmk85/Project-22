Our Story

Founded in 2026, Lumière Joaillerie was born from a passion for beauty and excellence in craftsmanship.

We believe that jewelry is more than just an accessory; it is an expression of personality, a marker of memory, and a symbol of love. Our artisans work tirelessly to create pieces that are not only visually stunning but also endure the test of time.

Every diamond is hand-selected, and every setting is precision-engineered to ensure that your Lumière piece shines as brightly as you do.
